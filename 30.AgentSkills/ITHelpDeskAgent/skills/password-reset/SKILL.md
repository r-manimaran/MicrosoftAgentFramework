## Instructions

1. Ask the employee for their username (e.g. john.doe@contoso.com).
2. Verify their identity by asking for their employee ID.
3. Direct them to the self-service-portal at https://resetpw.contoso.com.
4. If the portal is unavailable, read the `references/SECURITY_POLICY.md_` for manual reset procedures.
5. Remind them that passwords must be changed every 90 days per policy.
6. For MFA issues, escalate to security@contoso.com with ticket priority HIGH.
