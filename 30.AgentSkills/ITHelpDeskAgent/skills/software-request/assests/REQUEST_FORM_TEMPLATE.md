## Review & SLA Timelines

| Scenario                             | Timeline          |
|--------------------------------------|-------------------|
| Pre-approved software (catalog)      | Same day          |
| New software — free / open source    | 2–3 business days |
| New software — paid, cost < $500/yr  | 3–5 business days |
| New software — paid, cost ≥ $500/yr  | 5–7 business days (procurement) |
| SaaS / cloud tool (any cost)         | 5–7 business days (InfoSec review) |
| Enterprise license negotiation       | 2–4 weeks         |

---

## Important Notes

- **SaaS tools** that store company data require InfoSec sign-off regardless of cost.
- **Free trials** must still go through this process if they involve company data.
- Software must not be installed before approval — policy violation may result in 
  removal and disciplinary action per the Acceptable Use Policy (AUP-002).
- Questions? Contact it-requests@contoso.com or call the IT helpline: ext. 4357.
