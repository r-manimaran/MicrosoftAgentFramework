## Instructions

1. Ask the employees what software they need and the business justification.
2. Check if the software is in approved catalog (common approvals: VS Code, Postman, Slack, Team, Office 365).
3. For approved software: read `assets\REQUEST_FORM_TEMPLATE.md` and help the employee fill it out.
4. For unapproved software: explain the procurement review takes 5-7 business days and require manager sign-off.
5. Submit completed forms to it-requests@contoso.com with subject: [SW-REQUEST] <EmployeeName> - <SoftwareName>.
